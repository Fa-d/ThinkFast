## 6. Recommended Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)

1. **App Store Optimization**
   - Keyword research for "screen time," "app blocker," "digital wellbeing"
   - Compelling screenshots and preview video
   - Localized descriptions for key markets

2. **Build Anticipation**
   - Landing page with email capture
   - Social media presence
   - Beta testing with early adopters

### Phase 2: Launch (Month 1)

1. **Product Hunt launch**
2. **Reddit engagement** in r/productivity, r/digitalminimalism, r/androidapps
3. **Free marketing tactics** - blog posts, YouTube reviews

### Phase 3: Post-Launch Growth

1. **Track metrics aggressively** (retention is key)
2. **Iterate based on feedback**
3. **Consider small ad spend** ($100-500/month) once PMF is validated
