## Success Metrics to Track

After collecting responses, calculate:

1. **NPS Score** = (% Promoters - % Detractors)
   - Promoters: 9-10 rating
   - Passives: 7-8 rating
   - Detractors: 0-6 rating
   - **GOAL:** NPS > 40

2. **"Can't Live Without" Score** = % who would be "very sad" if app disappeared
   - **GOAL:** 70%+

3. **Weekly Opens** = % who opened app 4+ times per week
   - **GOAL:** 70%+

4. **Goal Setup Rate** = % who set up goals within 3 days
   - **GOAL:** 70%+

5. **Usage Reduction** = % who report 20%+ reduction
   - **GOAL:** 70%+

6. **Intervention Effectiveness**
   - Dismissal rate: 30%+
   - Helpful rating: 70%+
   - Freshness: 70%+

7. **Monetization Potential** = % willing to pay
   - **GOAL:** 30%+
